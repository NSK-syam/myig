How Instagram outfit search works

SearchOutfit extracts the public images from an Instagram post, lets users pick the correct slide when a carousel contains multiple looks, and analyzes the selected image for visible garments, accessories, color palette, and styling cues. That turns an ambiguous social post into a cleaner shopping intent.

Why use SearchOutfit instead of guessing product names

Most shoppers do not know what to type when they see a look on Instagram. SearchOutfit reduces that friction by identifying likely tops, trousers, dresses, jackets, shoes, bags, and accessories from the image itself, then surfacing merchant matches in the user’s preferred shopping country.
