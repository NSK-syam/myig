Frequently asked questions

Can I search from Instagram, screenshots, and image URLs?

Yes. SearchOutfit supports public Instagram posts, direct image URLs, uploaded screenshots, and pasted screenshots.

What does the outfit search tool return?

It analyzes the look, detects wearable items, and returns shoppable product matches with merchant links so you can move from inspiration to checkout quickly.

Does SearchOutfit support country-specific shopping results?

Yes. You can choose a preferred shopping country to bias the results toward more relevant merchants and pricing, while still exploring similar alternatives.
