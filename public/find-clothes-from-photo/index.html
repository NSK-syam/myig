How to find clothes from a photo with SearchOutfit

Shoppers often discover a look on social media, in a saved screenshot, or in a random image with no brand context. SearchOutfit closes that gap by reading the image itself. It identifies visible clothing and accessories, generates a structured outfit analysis, and uses those signals to search for similar products.

When photo-based outfit search is most useful

SearchOutfit works best when the outfit items are clearly visible and the shopper wants help identifying dresses, tops, jackets, trousers, shoes, bags, or accessories. It is especially useful for inspiration-led shopping, trend discovery, and cross-checking similar styles across different merchants and countries.

Frequently asked questions

Can SearchOutfit find clothes from a single photo?

Yes. A single image, screenshot, or public image URL is enough for SearchOutfit to attempt clothing and accessory detection.

Does country selection affect the results?

Yes. SearchOutfit lets shoppers choose a preferred country so merchant and pricing results are biased toward more relevant markets.
