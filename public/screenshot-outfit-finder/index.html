What makes screenshots useful for outfit search

Screenshots preserve the exact moment a shopper discovered a look. Instead of trying to describe a neckline, fabric, or silhouette from memory, SearchOutfit lets the shopper submit the saved image directly and use the visual evidence to identify similar tops, dresses, pants, jackets, shoes, and accessories.

How SearchOutfit handles screenshot-based shopping intent

SearchOutfit analyzes the visible garments in the screenshot, estimates item names and styling cues, then uses those signals to rank product matches. That is especially useful when the screenshot mixes editorial imagery, social media content, or a merchant page where the original product name is unknown to the shopper.

Can SearchOutfit find clothes from a screenshot?

Yes. Upload or paste a screenshot and SearchOutfit will attempt to identify the visible outfit items and return shoppable matches.
